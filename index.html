Foundation

Core Rules & Regulations

These ten fundamental rules form the backbone of our organization. Adherence is mandatory for all personnel regardless of rank or tenure.

01
⚡

Respect the Chain of Command

All members are expected to follow orders from higher-ranking personnel. Disrespect, insubordination, or refusal to follow lawful orders may result in disciplinary action. The chain exists to maintain order and operational efficiency.

02
🎖️

Professional Conduct

Members will maintain discipline and professionalism during all operations, meetings, and organizational activities. Your behavior reflects on the entire organization and its reputation.

03
📅

Activity Requirements

Members are expected to remain active and contribute to the organization. Extended inactivity without notice may result in demotion or removal. Communication is key — always notify leadership of extended absences.

04
🔒

Loyalty & Confidentiality

Internal information, operations, plans, and member information are not to be shared with outsiders. What happens within the organization stays within the organization. Breach of confidentiality is treated as a severe infraction.

05
🚫

No Unauthorized Actions

Members may not conduct operations, issue punishments, recruit personnel, or represent the organization without proper authorization. All actions must be approved through the appropriate chain of command.

06
🤝

Conflict Resolution

Internal disputes must be reported to the appropriate supervisor. Personal conflicts are not to interfere with organizational operations. Handle disagreements professionally and through proper channels.

07
⚖️

Abuse of Authority

Any rank found abusing their authority, issuing unfair punishments, or acting outside their responsibilities may face disciplinary action. Power comes with responsibility — use it wisely and justly.

08
📝

Recruitment Standards

All recruits must be properly evaluated before being accepted into the organization. Do not rush the vetting process. Quality over quantity — every member represents our standards.

09
🔐

Operational Security

Operational details are only shared on a need-to-know basis. Members must avoid discussing sensitive matters publicly. Loose lips sink ships — protect our operations at all costs.

10
🚨

Punishment Evasion

Attempting to avoid punishments, investigations, or disciplinary reviews will result in harsher penalties. Face consequences with integrity — evasion only compounds the problem.

Consequences

Disciplinary Actions

Violations of our rules are categorized into three tiers. Each tier carries a specific set of consequences designed to maintain order and fairness.

🟢 Minor Infractions

  • Verbal Warning
  • Written Warning
  • Temporary Restriction

Typically issued for first-time offenses, minor disrespect, or minor procedural violations.

🟡 Moderate Infractions

  • Probation
  • Rank Suspension
  • Demotion

Applied for repeated minor offenses, moderate unauthorized actions, or breaches of trust.

🔴 Severe Infractions

  • Rank Removal
  • Blacklisting
  • Permanent Expulsion

Reserved for confidentiality breaches, abuse of authority, espionage, or severe misconduct.

Hierarchy

Rank Structure

Our organization operates on a clear hierarchical system. Each rank carries specific responsibilities, authority levels, and expectations.

Leadership

Founders

Highest authority within the organization. Responsible for long-term direction and major decisions. Not to be contacted unless absolutely necessary.

  • Final decision-making authority
  • Organization-wide policy creation
  • Strategic planning and vision
  • Emergency override powers
High Authority

Head Enforcer

Supervises all Enforcers. Oversees punishments, promotions, and demotions. Creates and assigns missions, operations, and objectives. Reports directly to Leadership.

  • Supervises all Enforcers
  • Oversees disciplinary actions
  • Creates and assigns missions
  • Reports directly to Leadership
Very High Authority

General Manager (GM)

Maintains all records, files, and documentation. Reviews punishments, promotions, and demotions. Assists in planning missions and organizational objectives. Ensures administrative efficiency.

  • Maintains organizational records
  • Reviews all personnel actions
  • Assists in mission planning
  • Ensures administrative efficiency
High Authority

Enforcer

Handles investigations, disputes, and disciplinary matters. Enforces organizational rules. Assigns missions and tasks to members. Requests major punishments through the General Manager.

  • Handles investigations and disputes
  • Enforces organizational rules
  • Assigns missions to members
  • Requests major punishments through GM
Above Standard Command

Lieutenant (LT)

Supervises lower-ranking personnel. Approves standard operations. Conducts recruitment efforts. May issue minor disciplinary actions.

  • Supervises lower-ranking personnel
  • Approves standard operations
  • Conducts recruitment efforts
  • Issues minor disciplinary actions
Operational Command

Operations Manager

Organizes operations and events. Assigns duties and responsibilities during missions. Coordinates personnel and resources.

  • Organizes operations and events
  • Assigns duties during missions
  • Coordinates personnel and resources
  • Mission logistics oversight
Specialized Personnel

Driver

Provides transportation support. Assists operational teams with logistics and movement. Maintains readiness for deployment.

  • Provides transportation support
  • Assists with logistics and movement
  • Maintains readiness for deployment
  • Specialized operational support
Basic Membership

Soldier

Carries out assigned tasks and missions. Follows orders from superiors. Represents the backbone of organizational manpower. Lowest official rank — must earn trust, respect, and advancement through performance.

  • Carries out assigned tasks and missions
  • Follows orders from superiors
  • Backbone of organizational manpower
  • Earns advancement through performance
No Authority

Civilian

Not a member of the organization. No authority or access to organizational operations. All civilians must be vetted before any interaction with internal operations.

  • Not an organizational member
  • No authority or operational access
  • Must be vetted for interaction
  • Recruitment pool potential

Advancement

Promotion Requirements

Advancement through our ranks is earned, not given. Each promotion tier requires demonstrated competence, loyalty, and dedication.

🎖️ Soldier → Driver / Operations Staff

Entry-Level Advancement

  • Demonstrates loyalty to the organization and its leadership
  • Maintains consistent activity and participation
  • Completes assigned tasks successfully and on time
  • Shows initiative and willingness to learn
  • Follows all rules and regulations without incident
🚗 Driver / Operations Staff → Lieutenant

Leadership Track

  • Demonstrates natural leadership ability among peers
  • Maintains a strong disciplinary record with no infractions
  • Earns trust and respect from command personnel
  • Shows ability to mentor and guide lower ranks
  • Consistently exceeds expectations in role duties
⭐ Lieutenant → Enforcer

Command Track

  • Proven ability to manage and supervise personnel effectively
  • Consistent operational success in assigned missions
  • Recommendation from senior command (GM or Head Enforcer)
  • Demonstrates fair judgment and impartial decision-making
  • Deep understanding of all organizational rules and protocols
🛡️ Enforcer → General Manager

Administrative Track

  • Demonstrates exceptional administrative competence
  • Proven organizational leadership across multiple departments
  • Approval from both Head Enforcer and Leadership
  • Maintains meticulous records and documentation standards
  • Handles complex organizational matters with professionalism
📋 General Manager → Head Enforcer

Executive Track

  • Exceptional leadership demonstrated over extended period
  • Long-term dedication and unwavering loyalty to the organization
  • Direct appointment by Leadership (Founders)
  • Proven ability to command respect at all organizational levels
  • Strategic vision aligned with organizational goals
Culture

Core Values

Beyond rules and ranks, these values define who we are as an organization. They guide our decisions, shape our culture, and set us apart.

🤝

Integrity

We act with honesty and strong moral principles. Our word is our bond, and we hold ourselves accountable for our actions at all times.

⚡

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in everything we do. Mediocrity has no place here — we push boundaries and exceed expectations.

🛡️

Loyalty

We stand by our organization and our brothers. Betrayal is unforgivable — loyalty is the foundation upon which everything is built.

🎯

Discipline

We maintain self-control and follow protocols. Discipline separates professionals from amateurs — it is our greatest weapon.

🔥

Courage

We face challenges head-on without hesitation. Courage is not the absence of fear — it is action in spite of it.

🔗

Unity

We are stronger together. Individual talent wins games, but teamwork and intelligence win championships. We move as one.
